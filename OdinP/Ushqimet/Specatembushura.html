Specat e mbushura

Peppers stuffed with rice remain one of the most favorite recipes of Albanians, especially during the summer season.

It is one of the oldest and most delicious dishes of traditional Albanian cuisine.

Ideal for a delicious lunch and dinner meal, the stuffed peppers are fantastically combined,

with fresh seasonal salads but also with various flavors of Albanian cuisine.

Ingredients

  • 5-6 peppers for filling
  • 2 medium onions
  • 4-5 cups of rice
  • 1 cup of minced meat
  • salt, pepper
